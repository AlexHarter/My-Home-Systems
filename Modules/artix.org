* Format partitions
#+BEGIN_SRC shell
mkfs.ext4 -L ROOT /dev/sda2
mkfs.fat -F 32 /dev/sda1
fatlabel /dev/sda1 BOOT
mkswap -L SWAP /dev/sda3
#+END_SRC
